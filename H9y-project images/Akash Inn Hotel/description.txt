This project focuses on creating a warm, inviting, and stylish interior environment for AKASH INN BOUTIQUE HOTEL, located in the heart of Shivamoga. The design emphasizes comfort and elegance across key areas, including:

Cosy Guest Rooms: Thoughtfully designed to provide a restful and luxurious experience, with modern furnishings, ambient lighting, and soothing color palettes.
Lobby: An inviting space that welcomes guests with a blend of contemporary aesthetics and local cultural elements, setting a warm tone for their stay.
Restaurant & Banquet Hall: A versatile dining and event space featuring elegant decor, comfortable seating, and an ambiance suitable for both leisure and business gatherings.
Attached Bar: A stylish bar area designed for relaxation and socialization, complementing the overall hospitality experience.
The interior design aims to cater to both leisure and business travelers by creating a harmonious balance between functionality and aesthetic appeal, ensuring a memorable stay for all guests.
